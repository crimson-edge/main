The Challenge

The primary challenge was to acquire customers profitably in the saturated trading education market, especially when the front-end product sells for less than five dollars.

The Solution

Crimson Edge Ventures implemented a complete system to not only acquire customers cheaply but also to maximize their lifetime value:

  • A conversion-optimized funnel was built in GoHighLevel with stacked bonuses and an authority video.
  • Precision media buying on Meta used look-alike audiences, interest stacking, and a weekly creative refresh.
  • An automated back-end system moved leads and buyers into segmented email and webinar sequences to sell high-ticket programs.
