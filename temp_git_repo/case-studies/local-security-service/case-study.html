Case Study: Local Private Security Service

This security services client needed to generate a steady flow of qualified bookings without overspending. Their previous efforts lacked filtering, resulting in wasted ad spend and time. Crimson Edge Ventures was hired to build a system that delivered results.

The Challenge

The client's primary goal was to fill their calendar with qualified prospects while staying below their target cost of $200 per appointment. Their existing funnel had no qualification process, leading to wasted resources on poor-fit leads.

The Solution

Crimson Edge Ventures built a comprehensive lead generation system using Google Ads and an intelligent booking funnel. Key strategies included:

  • Targeting high-intent 'near me' and emergency security keywords on Google Search.
  • Using 'Call Only' ads to capture mobile users ready to speak immediately.
  • Developing a quote form with automated SMS and email follow-ups to reduce no-shows.
  • Performing weekly bid adjustments to continuously drive down costs.
