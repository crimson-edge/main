Case Study: Financial Education Company

This financial education company engaged Crimson Edge Ventures to scale their "Ultimate Day Trading Checklist." In thirty days, the team launched a micro-purchase funnel and Meta ad campaigns that landed buyers at under $40, setting up a 4-to-1 return on ad spend.

The Challenge

The goal was to acquire low-cost buyers for a $4.95 checklist and then convert them through a webinar into a $2,495 high-ticket offer, all while staying cash-positive throughout the process.

The Solution

Crimson Edge Ventures designed a multi-stage funnel to acquire buyers and systematically upsell them:

  • A high-converting GoHighLevel funnel was built, offering the checklist along with $1,700 in stacked bonuses to increase the offer's value.
  • Meta ad campaigns utilized look-alike audiences and interest stacking, with creatives refreshed weekly to maintain performance.
  • An automated email and webinar sequence moved new buyers into a live presentation for the $2,495 premium offer.
